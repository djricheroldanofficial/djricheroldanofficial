DjRicheRoldan

Biography

Riche Roldan is a music producer and a DJ in General Santos City. He has done a lot of party music and he has also done a lot of party events in Mindanao. He started in this field in 2010 and now he still remains in this field and the music that he has made is expanding. He has been inspired by the big sound systems here in Mindanao since he started in this field.

DjRicheRoldan has also been a member of popular music producers clubs here in the Philippines such as Power Beats Club, Gensan Mix Club, Davao Mix Club.
